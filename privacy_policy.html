Privacy Policy

My Weight Diary  ·  Effective Date: 20 June 2026  ·  Last Updated: 20 June 2026

The short version: My Weight Diary stores all your data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or share your personal information with anyone. Your data is yours.

My Weight Diary ("the App") is developed and maintained by ModmanTV ("we", "our", or "us"). If you have any questions about this policy, you can reach us at:

  • Email: modmancreative@gmail.com

2. What Data the App Collects

The App allows you to record and track personal health-related information, including:

  • Body weight measurements and associated dates
  • Personal notes and diary entries
  • Progress photos (taken or selected from your device)
  • App settings and customisation preferences
  • Reminder and notification preferences

All of this data is stored exclusively on your device. We do not operate servers that receive or store your information, and we have no access to any data you enter into the App.

3. How Your Data Is Stored

Your data is stored in two ways on your device:

  • Local database: Weight entries, notes, and diary data are saved in an on-device SQLite database managed by Android Room. This database is private to the App and cannot be accessed by other apps without your permission.
  • Local file storage: Progress photos are saved to the App's private storage directory on your device. App settings and preferences are saved using Android DataStore, also stored locally.

4. Backup and Export

The App includes an optional backup and restore feature. When you choose to export a backup, the App creates a ZIP file containing your data. This file is saved to a location on your device that you select. We do not receive a copy of this file, and it is not uploaded anywhere automatically. You are solely responsible for the security of any exported backup files.

5. Permissions We Request

The App may request the following Android permissions:

  • Camera / Media / Photos: To allow you to take or select progress photos. Photos are stored locally and are never uploaded.
  • Notifications / Alarms: To deliver locally scheduled reminders you set up within the App. These notifications are triggered entirely on your device with no server involvement.
  • Storage (on older Android versions): To save and read backup files to/from your chosen location.

You can manage or revoke permissions at any time through your device's Settings app. Revoking a permission may disable related features in the App.

6. Analytics, Advertising, and Third Parties

The App does not include any analytics SDKs, advertising networks, crash reporting services, or any other third-party code that collects or transmits your data. No third party receives any information about you or your usage of this App.

7. Internet Access

My Weight Diary does not require an internet connection to function. The App does not make network requests and does not communicate with any remote servers under any circumstances.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because all data stays on your device and we collect nothing, there is no data for us to access or delete regardless of the user's age. If you believe a child has used the App and you have concerns, please contact us at modmancreative@gmail.com.

9. Your Rights and Data Control

Because your data is stored entirely on your device, you have full and direct control over it at all times. You can:

  • Access your data — it is visible within the App at all times.
  • Export your data — use the built-in backup/export feature to obtain a copy.
  • Delete your data — delete individual entries within the App, or clear all data by going to your device Settings → Apps → My Weight Diary → Clear Data, or by uninstalling the App.

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or the United Kingdom and have questions about your rights under GDPR or UK GDPR, please contact us at modmancreative@gmail.com. Given that we hold no data about you on our end, most GDPR rights are exercised directly on your device.

10. Security

Your data resides in your device's private app storage, which is protected by Android's standard security model. We encourage you to use a screen lock on your device and to keep your Android operating system up to date to maintain the security of your data. We cannot be held responsible for unauthorised access resulting from a compromised device.

We encourage you to review this page periodically.
